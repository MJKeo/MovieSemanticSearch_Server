# Additional objective notes - Adaptation source flag

Search for the origin-medium flag of the story: novel, short story, stage,
comic, folklore, video game, TV source, true story, biography, or remake.
Emit the source type only; named authors and named franchises are separate
traits.

Pick by where the story came from, not by what appears in the story.
"Based on true events" is true-story. "A Lincoln biopic" is biography.
"Stephen King novels" emits novel here; Stephen King belongs to Named
source creator.

`REMAKE` means retelling an earlier film as an origin source. Franchise-
scoped remake phrasing ("the Scarface remake") belongs to franchise
lineage instead.

No-fire when no source-medium cue is present, when the phrase is only a
story subject ("about JFK"), or when a bare franchise happens to have an
adapted origin but the user did not ask for that origin.
