# Additional objective notes - Country of origin

## Target

Fire for legal/financial production origin: produced in, made by, national
production, co-production.

## Decision Questions

- Is the country word about production origin?
- Is it instead about filming location, spoken language, or cinema tradition?
- If region-level wording appears, can it be expanded from the wording alone?

## Boundaries

- "Produced in France" and "British production" fire here.
- "Shot in Iceland" is filming location.
- "Spanish-language" is audio language.
- "Bollywood", "Korean cinema", and named movements are cultural tradition.
- Bare nationality phrases are ambiguous; prefer this only when production
  framing is explicit.
