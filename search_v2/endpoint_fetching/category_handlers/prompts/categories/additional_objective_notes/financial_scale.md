# Additional objective notes - Financial scale

## Target

Fire for budget scale, box-office scale, or compact financial labels that
combine both.

## Decision Questions

- Is the financial surface budget, gross, or both?
- Do the columns reinforce one concept ("blockbuster") or is only one axis
  stated ("low-budget", "flop")?
- Is any quality/status wording a separate sibling trait?

## Boundaries

- "Blockbuster" can mean big budget plus big gross.
- "Indie hit" can mean small budget plus strong gross.
- "Flop" is box-office performance, not quality by itself.
- "Cult classic", "underrated", "crowd-pleaser", and "popular" are not
  financial scale unless the financial axis is explicit.
