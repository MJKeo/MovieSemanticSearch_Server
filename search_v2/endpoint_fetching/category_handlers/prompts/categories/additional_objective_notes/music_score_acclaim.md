# Additional objective notes - Music / score acclaim

Search praise or recognition for a film's music, score, soundtrack, songs,
theme, or sound of the musical identity. Use semantic reception.

This category is about how the music is regarded, not who composed it and
not whether the film is a musical. Named composers belong to person credit.
Musical genre/form belongs to genre or keyword categories.

Phrase the reception body as compact praised qualities: "iconic score",
"memorable soundtrack", "beloved theme", "music praised as haunting".

No-fire for named composer credit, music documentaries as subject matter,
musicals as genre, or vague "good sound" when the target is not music/score.
