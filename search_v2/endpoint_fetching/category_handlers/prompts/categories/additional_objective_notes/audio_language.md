# Additional objective notes - Audio language

## Target

Fire for spoken/audio language requirements: original language, dubbed
language, or subtitled/non-English audio.

## Decision Questions

- Does the phrase explicitly target what language is heard?
- Is the country word about audio, production origin, or cinema tradition?
- Can the language be taken from the input without outside assumptions?

## Boundaries

- "Korean-language" fires here. "Korean cinema" does not.
- "Made in France" is country of origin.
- "Bollywood" / "French New Wave" are cultural tradition.
- Do not infer audio language from nationality, setting, cast, or genre.
