# Additional objective notes - Viewing occasion

Search concrete situations for watching: date night, rainy Sunday, long
flight, family movie night, background watch, first movie with kids.
Use semantic `watch_context.watch_scenarios`.

The occasion is an external situation, not the movie's internal tone or
emotional effect. "For a date night" belongs here; "romantic", "uplifting",
or "tense" belongs elsewhere unless the retrieval_intent frames it as the
occasion's practical need.

Keep the body as short scenario phrases. Do not fill viewer_experience,
plot, or metadata-like fields from this category.

No-fire for pure mood, genre, audience pitch, content-safety, or vague
"something good to watch" phrasing without a concrete situation.
