# Additional objective notes - Award records

Search formal award records: tracked ceremonies, named prizes, category
tags, outcomes, award years, and count/intensity requirements.

Read the award condition before filling axes. Specific filters usually use
binary floor scoring. Generic recognition strength ("award-winning",
"heavily decorated", "most decorated") uses threshold scoring because more
records should help.

Winner/nominee follows wording. Bare ceremony or festival references mean
recognition unless the outcome is explicit. Generic "award-winning" means
winner; generic "award-nominated" means nominee.

Only tracked ceremonies and prizes can fire. Do not substitute close
ceremonies. Razzies fire only when explicitly named, never from generic
"bad" or "worst" quality language.

No-fire for general acclaim, canon/list membership, popularity, or vague
"gets respect" phrasing with no award vocabulary.
