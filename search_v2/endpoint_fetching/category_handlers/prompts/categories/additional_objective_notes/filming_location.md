# Additional objective notes - Filming location

Search where the camera was: physical production geography. Use semantic
`production.filming_locations` with compact place names.

The target is a shooting location, not story setting, legal production
country, language, or national cinema tradition. "Filmed in Tokyo" belongs
here; "set in Tokyo" does not.

Populate only production filming locations. Do not add plot, reception,
watch-context, or production-technique bodies to make the query look richer.

No-fire for vague scenic language with no place, country-of-origin phrasing,
cultural-tradition phrasing, or narrative-setting phrasing.
