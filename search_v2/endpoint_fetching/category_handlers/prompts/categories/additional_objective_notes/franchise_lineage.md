# Additional objective notes - Franchise / universe lineage

Search for membership or position inside a named franchise, universe,
series, subgroup, or lineage. A resolvable anchor is load-bearing.

Fire when the request names a franchise/universe, recognized subgroup,
mainline/offshoot distinction, sequel/prequel/reboot/remake position, or
launch/crossover role tied to a specific lineage.

Generic structural flags without an anchor do not belong here. "Remakes",
"sequels", and "spinoffs" by themselves route elsewhere unless
retrieval_intent supplies the named franchise anchor.

Use the narrowest literal scope. A narrow lineage should not be OR-expanded
back to its parent umbrella. Studio names as producers, character-only
mentions, genres, and source-medium flags are out of scope.
