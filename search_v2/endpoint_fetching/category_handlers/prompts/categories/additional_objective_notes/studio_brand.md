# Additional objective notes - Studio / brand

Search for the production entity behind the film: studio, label, or brand.
This is about who made it, not where it streams and not what franchise it
belongs to.

Use the curated brand path for umbrella brands the registry covers at the
scope the user means. Use freeform names for specific sub-labels, historical
or foreign studios, and long-tail companies absent from the registry.

Do not simulate brand history with year filters or extra aliases. The brand
registry owns rename chains and time-bounded ownership. Pick the brand level
that matches the request.

No-fire for availability phrasing ("on Netflix"), vague industrial classes
("indie studios", "major studios"), named persons, or franchise/universe
identity ("MCU", "Star Wars") unless the wording is explicitly production
company framing.
