# Additional objective notes - General appeal / quality baseline

## Target

Fire for broad quality, reception, or popularity priors without a specific
numeric cutoff or cultural-status shape.

## Decision Questions

- Is this a general lift, not a hard threshold?
- Does the wording imply reception quality, static popularity, or both?
- Is another category responsible for the specific axis?

## Boundaries

- "Well-received", "highly regarded", "great", and "popular" fire here.
- Explicit numbers go to Numeric reception score.
- "Classic", "cult", "underrated", "divisive", and "era-defining" are
  Cultural status.
- "Trending now" is live Trending, not static popularity.
- For "best horror of the 80s", handle only the broad quality prior; genre
  and era are sibling traits.
